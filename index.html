About the Game

"Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock" is a variation of the classic Rock Paper Scissors game, created by Sheldon Cooper from the TV show *The Big Bang Theory*. The game introduces two additional gestures: Lizard and Spock, adding complexity to the decision-making process.

About The Big Bang Theory

*The Big Bang Theory* is a popular American sitcom that follows the lives of four socially awkward scientists and their interactions with each other and the world around them. One of the show's most famous characters, Sheldon Cooper, introduces the game of "Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock" in Season 2, Episode 8.
